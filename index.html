The Why

Why We're
Building This

Over the next decade, thousands of small business owners across Appalachia will retire. Most built something real — a customer base, a skilled workforce, a reputation earned over decades. And most have no one to hand it to.

When those businesses go to the wrong buyer, the jobs follow the capital out of the region. When they close, the community feels it for a generation.

We grew up watching that happen. We're building Kanawha Partners because we believe there's a better way — one where those businesses don't just survive the transition, but come out the other side stronger with better systems, more stability, and a future their employees can count on.

It's just what good ownership looks like.
